PG County Spending FY2022, Payments by Agency Paid

  • The following visualization describes payments to different agencies of PG county in the 2022 fiscal year.
  • This includes amount of payments per agency, sum of payments per agency, and the amount of payments to particular payees.
  • Hover over each bar for each data point and interact with the filtering options below (*ahem* there's a lot of police spending).

  • *** It is to be noted this is from a sample size of 1000 records, the original dataset has far more, yet were inaccessible due to issues with the PG county API.
  • Please take this into consideration when examining the data, and visit the link in the footer for access to the complete data set ***
